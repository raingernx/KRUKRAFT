## What Is Krukraft?

**Krukraft** is a Thai SaaS marketplace for downloadable digital learning resources.

- **Product type:** SaaS marketplace for downloadable learning resources
- **Primary market:** Thailand (Thai teachers and students)
- **Creator economy angle:** Teachers create and sell study materials (worksheets, PDFs, templates, etc.)
- **Monetization:** Resource sales (paid/free), with Stripe and Xendit payment integrations
- **Platform comparisons referenced:** Gumroad, Creative Market, Notion, Canva, Etsy

## Name Origin

- **Kru** = ครู = teacher (Thai word)
- **Craft** = creative, handmade, creator economy (Etsy/Creative Market vibes)
- Combined = "Teachers' creative marketplace" — a platform where Thai teachers craft and sell educational resources

## History

- **Previous names:** PaperDock and StudyPlatform
- **Interim brand/domain:** KruCraft / `krucrafts.com`
- **Current canonical brand/domain:** Krukraft / `krukraft.com`
